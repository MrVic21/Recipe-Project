Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe!

Home Page

Description:

This is my recipe of choice when it comes to chocolate chip cookies. The second best cookies to make after peanut butter. These cookies are on the chewy gooey side of the spectrum!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of butter softened
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps to Success:

  1. Grab all ingredients. Butter should be soft and eggs around room tempature (No Cold Eggs).
  2. Preheat Oven to 350 Fahrenheit (175 celsius). Beat all butter and sugarsin a bowl till smooth.
  3. Beat eggs into the bowl 1 at a time and then add vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in water then add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips and walnuts.
  6. Drop 2 inch spoonfuls of cookie dough on a ungreased baking sheet or grease pan.
  7. Bake in preheated oven until outsides are brown. Around 10 minutes.
  8. Let sit on a baking rack to cool down
  9. Eat or store in cold storage and enjoy!
