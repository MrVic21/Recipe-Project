Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home Page

Description:

This is my recipe of choice when it comes to chocolate chip cookies. The second best cookies to make after peanut butter. These cookies are on the chewy gooey side of the spectrum!

Ingredients:

  1. 1. 1 cup of butter softened
  2. 2. 1 cup of white sugar
  3. 3. 1 cup packed brown sugar
  4. 4. 2 large eggs
  5. 5. 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  6. 6. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  7. 7. 2 teaspoons hot water
  8. 8. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  9. 9. 3 cups all purpose flour
  10. 10. 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
  11. 11. 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
