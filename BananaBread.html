Banana Bread

Banana Bread Recipe

Home Page

Description:

This is my banana bread recipe of choice. Among the most soft and sweet I've tried.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps to Success

  1. Gather ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheit (175 celsius). Grease 9x5 loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda and salt in a bowl.
  3. Beat butter and sugar until smooth then all stirred eggs and bananas.
  4. Stir batter into flour.
  5. Pour batter into greased loaf pan
  6. Bake in oven till toothpick goes in and out clean. Around 60 minutes.
  7. Let sit on baking rake until cool then enjoy!
