Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Home Page

Description:

By far one of my favorite dinner's to have. The classic Chicken Alfredo.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound uncooked fettucini/linguine
  • 2 chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 16 oz of alfredo pasta sauce
  • 10 oz of frozen veggies
  • 4.5 oz of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup of milk

Steps to Success

  1. Large salted pot to boil with water and cook pasta for 8 minutes the drain
  2. Put cubed chicken, veggies, sauce and milk into a sauce pan on medium-low heat till ingredients are tender
  3. Put finished noodles into pan and stir together
  4. Wait to cool down and enjoy!
